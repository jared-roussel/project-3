Project 3

We propose to illustrate and conclude on the correlation between population over time and the cost of living in various cities in the United States. We plan to draw direct comparisons in city populations as well as their cost of living in those cities.

Get Started

Project Table

Data Sources: US Census Data (data.gov), Cost of Living API, Super Bowl Host Cities (Kaggle), Fortune 500 Corporate Headquarters, World Cities (Kaggle). Table

Map

This section of our project allows users to input longitude and latitude coordinantes of cities to locate them on a Map.

Population Change

This section of our project renders a Bar Chart representing ten years of census records depicting population change from 2010-2020 in major cities across the United States.

Family Finances

This section of our project illustrates the cost of living, local purchasing power, cost of groceries, price of rent and restaurant rates for major cities in the United States with a Line Graph.

Fortune 500

This section of our project illustrates a line Graph representing the average fortune 500 company rank, total amount of employees, total revenue, total profit and the total amount of fortune 500 companies located in major cities in the United States.
